Welcome to the coffee shop, a sort of café and shop in one. Here you can enjoy a coffee in the ambient atmosphere here in the shop or buy one of the brand of beans we have in stock that you can take home to enjoy.

If you come to our café though, why not have a coffee here and take a seat in one of the large armchairs we have and enjoy. We have everything here to help you to unwind with coffee, the loveliest deserts to induldge with board games to play if you come with company. If you’re by yourself, help yourself to reading a daily newspaper or read one of the books by our smug library. It's all up to you.

We are sure you will enjoy the coffee here at Aroma Coffee.
